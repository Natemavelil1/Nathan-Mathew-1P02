Bio

My father bought the old Canon Powershot G9 and back then I never understood why he used to force everyone to stand

and take a picture before everyone left. Looking back now with all the pictures saved on my computer, those pictures are timeless and brings back so many memories.

My goal as a photographer is to be as creative as possible and bring new meaning to the ordinary. My favorite type of photography is landscape as I have always felt one with nature.
